Packages Information

Our All-Inclusive Package ensures that every guest enjoys an unforgettable experience surrounded by nature and indulgence. Savor an ultra-romantic 5-course gourmet dinner in our elegant European-style dining room — or opt for intimate take-out room service to dine in privacy. Unwind in your private outdoor hot tub, perfectly positioned on the lake’s edge, and cozy up beside a real wood-burning fireplace with complimentary firewood and firestarter provided. You’re welcome to bring your own favorite wine for dinner or your special bubbles for the in-room whirlpool bath to make your stay uniquely yours. Located just moments from Algonquin Park, you’ll find endless opportunities for fresh air, wildlife encounters, scenic trails, tranquil lakes, and visits to local museums and artist galleries. The perfect escape for couples seeking romance, relaxation, and connection.

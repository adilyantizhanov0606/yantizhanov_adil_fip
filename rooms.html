Rooms Information

Our rooms exceed 5 Diamond/Star Room Standards, offering the perfect blend of luxury, comfort, and natural beauty. Enjoy a private outdoor hot tub and a spacious lakeside view from your walkout balcony or deck. Each room features a King Size bed, wood-burning fireplace, and a European shower with two or more heads. Stay connected with free fibre-optic high-speed Wi-Fi, and unwind with a 50-inch television, Keurig coffee maker with K-caps, bar fridge, safe, hair dryer and cosmetic mirror, iron and ironing board, two plush bathrobes, and an alarm clock. Relax in a two-person whirlpool bath tub or on two chaise lounge chairs (available spring through fall), while enjoying pure drinking water from the Algonquin Park aquifer. Complimentary free long-distance calling completes your luxurious and worry-free stay.
